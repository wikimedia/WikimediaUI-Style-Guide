Events

Events we attend

Research team members regularly attend, present, and publish at scholarly research and Wikimedia Movement conferences, hackathons, and workshops.

  • CSCW: ACM Conference on Computer-Supported Cooperative Work and Social Computing
  • CHI: ACM Conference on Human Factors in Computing Systems
  • ICWSM: AAAI International Conference on Web and Social Media
  • Wikimania: Wikimedia Foundation Annual Conference
  • Wikimedia Hackathon: Wikimedia Foundation Annual Hackathon
  • WWW: International World Wide Web Conference

Event series we run

Wikimedia Research runs re-occuring events devoted to furthering and disseminating scholarship around Wikimedia projects and open collaboration.

  • Wikimedia Research Showcase: A monthly public showcase of recent research by Research team members, collaborators, and guest presenters from the scholarly research community.
  • Wiki Workshop: An annual forum hosted at a major scholarly conference bringing together researchers exploring all aspects of Wikipedia and other Wikimedia sites.
  • WikiCite Conference: An annual scholarly conference that brings together librarians, modelers, Wikimedia contributors and open data stakeholders to develop a centralized bibliographic database of source and citation metadata in Wikidata to serve Wikimedia projects and, eventually, the sum of all human knowledge
