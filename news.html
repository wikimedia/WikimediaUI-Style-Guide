News

Follow Wikimedia Research

You can keep up with news and research activities of the Research team, as well as other research within the Wikimedia Foundation and the community, through the following channels:

  • @WikiResearch: The Twitter handle for the Wikimedia research community, covering updates on new studies, tools and datasets hot off the press.
  • Research on the Wikimedia Blog: Announcements and reports about Wikimedia Research projects as well as research news from across the movement.
  • Research Newsletter: A monthly newsletter edited by volunteer contributors and Research team members that features both internal research at the Wikimedia Foundation and other peer-reviewed research on Wikimedia projects.
  • Research mailing list: A public mailing list for discussions of research on Wikimedia projects.
