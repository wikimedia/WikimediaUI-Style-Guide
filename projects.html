Research projects

Wikimedia researchers support Wikimedia’s mission by performing research that informs product development and increases our understanding of the global community of readers and contributors.

Featured Projects

  1. Increasing Diversity

    Engaging and retaining contributors from underrepresented groups to increase the diversity of Wikimedia projects.

  2. Structured Citations

    Building a structured repository of citable sources that can serve Wikipedia and other open knowledge projects.

  3. Structured Multimedia

    Understanding the impact of structured data on the curation and discoverability of multimedia content.

  4. Scoring Platform

    Enhancing the impact of machine classifiers for quality control while minimizing their detrimental effects.

  5. Knowledge Gaps

    Developing systems to identify content gaps across Wikipedia languages and recommend contributions based on editors’ interests.

  6. Why We Read Wikipedia

    Studying the motivation and information-seeking behavior of Wikipedia readers through surveys and navigation data.
