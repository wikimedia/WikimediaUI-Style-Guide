About

Research collaborators

Wikimedia Research is part of a broader community of scholars and students of Wikimedia projects and online collaboration in general. Collaboration with researchers inside and outside of the Wikimedia Foundation – from academia and industry – is what keeps our research environment vibrant.

Current collaborators include researchers from institutions such as Cornell University, Jigsaw (Google), Stanford University, and the University of Minnesota. You can view a full list of our current collaborators on the collaborators page.

If you are interested in learning more about our formal collaboration process, please read our formal collaboration FAQ, or email the research team at research-wmf [at] lists.wikimedia.org.
